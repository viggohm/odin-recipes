Lasagna Bolognese

A traditionally made lasagna bolognese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • Tomato paste
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 glass red wine
  • ½ liter beef stock
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • 3½ tablespoon flour
  • 50g butter
  • 7dl whole milk
  • Nutmeg
  • 100g parmesan
  • 200g shredded mozzarella
  • lasagna sheets

Directions

Meat sauce

  1. Finely cut the vegetables
  2. Saute the vegetables
  3. Saute the meat
  4. Roast the tomato paste
  5. Add the liquids
  6. Add the meat and vegetables
  7. Simmer for as long as possible
  8. Add species and season with salt and pepper

Cheese sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a pot
  2. Add flour and roast for 5 minutes
  3. Add milk little by little until the slightly thinner than desired
  4. Add the cheese and boil for additional 5 minutes
  5. Season with nutmeg, salt and pepper

Assembling the lasagna

  1. Put a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of the dish
  2. Continue with 3 layers for sheets, meat sauce, and cheese sauce ending with a thicker layer of cheese sauce
  3. Bake in the oven at 200 degrees celsius until done (approx. 30 minutes)
  4. Rest the lasagna for 10 minutes and enjoy
