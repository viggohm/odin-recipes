Pizza Margherita

Simple, pure, pizza

A traditionally made Pizza Margherita

Ingredients

  • Dough ball
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Basil

Directions

  1. Stretch the dough to the desired size of pizza
  2. Add tomato sauce
  3. Spread mozzarella pieces
  4. Cook until done, preferably in a pizza oven or on baking steel
  5. Add 3 leaves of basil
